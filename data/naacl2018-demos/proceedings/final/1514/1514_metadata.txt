Abstract#==#We demonstrate ELISA-EDL, a state-of-the-art re-trainable system to extract
entity mentions from low-resource languages, link them to external English
knowledge bases, and visualize locations related to disaster topics on a world
heatmap. We make all of our data sets, resources and system training and
testing APIs publicly available for research purpose.

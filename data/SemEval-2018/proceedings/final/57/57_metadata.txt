Abstract#==#This paper describes the participation of ELiRF-UPV team at task 10, Capturing
Discriminative Attributes, of SemEval-2018. Our best approach consists of using
ConceptNet, Wikipedia and NumberBatch embeddings in order to stablish
relationships between concepts and attributes. Furthermore, this system
achieves competitive results in the official evaluation.

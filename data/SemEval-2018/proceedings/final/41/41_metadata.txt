Abstract#==#This paper describes our approach to SemEval-2018 Task1: Estimation of Affects
in Tweet for 1a and 2a. 
Our team KDE-AFFECT employs several methods including one-dimensional
Convolutional Neural Network for $n$-grams, together with word embedding and
other preprocessing such as vocabulary unification and Emoji conversion into
four emotional words.

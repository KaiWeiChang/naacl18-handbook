Abstract#==#Emotion classification is a new task that combines several disciplines
including Artificial Intelligence and Psychology, although Natural Language
Processing is perhaps the most challenging area. In this paper, we describe our
participation in SemEval-2018 Task1: Affect in Tweets. In particular, we have
participated in EI-oc, EI-reg and E-c subtasks for English and Spanish languages.

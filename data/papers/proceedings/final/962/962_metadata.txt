Abstract#==#We present a corpus of 240 argumentative essays written by non-native speakers
of English annotated for metaphor. The corpus is  made publicly available. We
provide benchmark performance of state-of-the-art systems on this new corpus,
and explore the relationship between writing proficiency and metaphor use.

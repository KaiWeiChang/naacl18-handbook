Abstract#==#We present a neural model for question generation from knowledge graphs triples
in a "Zero-shot" setup, that is generating questions for predicate, subject
types or object types that were not seen at training time.
Our model leverages triples occurrences in the natural language corpus in a
encoder-decoder architecture, paired with an original part-of-speech copy
action mechanism to generate questions.
Benchmark and human evaluation show that our model outperforms state-of-the-art
on this task.

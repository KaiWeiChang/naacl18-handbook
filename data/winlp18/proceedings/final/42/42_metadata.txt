Abstract#==#In this paper, we present our data collection process, annotation schema and
agreement results for extracting health goals from SMS conversations between a
health coach and the patients. This is our first step towards building an
autonomous virtual assistant health coach that learns from expert demonstration
to interact with patients via SMS.

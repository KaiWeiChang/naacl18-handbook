Abstract#==#In this project we computationally examine the factors underlying codeswitching
in English-Swahili conversations. We utilize the integration index (I-index),
which is the ratio switch points to words and gives us insight into how people
are responding to each others codeswitching behavior. Our analysis shows how
the word choice at switch points is affected by the topic of the conversation.
